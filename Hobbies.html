MY HOBBIES

As the eldest among my siblings, I play a significant role in setting an example and taking on responsibilities within my family. My hobbies, which include playing Mobile Legends, cleaning, and cooking, reflect a diverse range of my interests and skills that contribute to my personal growth and the well-being of our family. Playing Mobile Legends is fun and helps me with problem-solving, decision-making, and teamwork. It's not just entertaining but also teaches me to work well with others. I enjoy cleaning and keeping things tidy, which shows my attention to detail and discipline. A clean environment helps us stay productive and happy. Cooking is another passion of mine, where I get to be creative and show my care for my family by preparing meals that bring us together.
